## Research fit

Projects are organised around two connected questions:

- **Heterogeneous intelligence:** how models learn and collaborate across different data, institutions, architectures, sensors and devices.
- **Terminal intelligence:** how useful capability is adapted to clinical, robotic and embedded terminals with limited compute, memory and latency.

Applications include trustworthy medical AI, federated learning, efficient foundation-model adaptation, image-guided intervention and heterogeneous robotic systems. Relevant backgrounds include machine learning, computer vision, medical image analysis, robotics, optimisation and distributed systems. Applicants should first read the [research directions](/research/) and identify a specific question or project that connects with their experience.
