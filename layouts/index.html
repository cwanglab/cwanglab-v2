Research framework

Cloud–Edge–Terminal intelligence

Cloud

Population and foundation models

Hospitals and biobanks hold imaging data at population scale. We build large-scale measurement, synthesis and representation-learning methods there — for example, the UK Biobank bone marrow imaging pipeline behind our Nature Communications studies.

Edge

Heterogeneous collaboration

Hospitals and partners cannot always pool data or run identical hardware. Federated learning and knowledge transfer let models learn across institutions, architectures and devices while source data stays under local governance.

Terminal

Intelligence at the point of use

Scanners, surgical systems and robots run on fixed hardware with strict time budgets. Efficient adaptation and compression put large-model capability onto the clinical and robotic terminals where it is used.
